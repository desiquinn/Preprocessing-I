Send Me A Message My Story In the early 90s my grandmother introduced me to coding. I was just 12 years old when I taught myself HTML and CSS. When I went to college I landed my first Jr. Web Designer job at Western Carolina University. Later I was recruited to design and manage the website for WCU's Women's Center. Eventually I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Biology and began working in medicine, research, and sales. Still something was missing in my life. After some thought I realized that Web Development had been my true passion all along. I am not a Lambda School student modernizing my exsisting skills and learning new ones to become a full stack web developer.
